I will be[presenting the Onion Architecture](http://www.onetug.org/Events/13-01-05/Onion_Architecture_with_Jack_Pines.aspx) and my implementation of the pattern at the upcoming [Orlando .NET Users Group (ONETUG)](http://www.onetug.org/) meeting. The talk will introduce the architecture, discuss its merits over traditional n-layer architecture, and demonstrate its ease of use, including the [Templify](http://opensource.endjin.com/templify/) package. If you're in Orlando on 01/17/2012, come cheer me on or razz me. Either way, fun will be had by someone. :-)
