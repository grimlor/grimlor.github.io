In Satya's book, ["Hit Refresh"](https://www.amazon.com/Hit-Refresh-Rediscover-Microsofts-Everyone-ebook/dp/B01HOT5SQA/), he discusses this quite a lot but I have up on my peg board what I find fundamental about why culture is so powerful.
